# Bi-directional Resonance Anchoring: Escaping the Pooling Paradox in High-Resolution Image and Video MLLMs

## Abstract
Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs) frequently suffer from hallucinations, generating text disconnected from visual or temporal evidence due to overwhelming language priors. Existing hidden-state interventions often fall into the "Pooling Paradox": to apply unified directional corrections, they aggregate thousands of high-resolution spatial or temporal tokens, inadvertently destroying the fine-grained 2D/3D positional coordinates necessary for dense grounding (e.g., Document VQA) and specific frame localization (e.g., Video Action). In this paper, we propose the mathematical framework and evaluation blueprint for **Bi-directional Resonance Anchoring (BRA)**, a decode-time intervention operating directly in the terminal logits space via localized visual matching. 

To resolve the compute and memory-bandwidth bottlenecks inherent in zero-pooling logits interventions, BRA introduces three systemic upgrades: (1) **Dual-Track Modality Calibration ($\Phi$)**, establishing a zero-shot identity mapping ($\text{BRA}_{zero}$) explicitly validated via representation sanity checks, alongside an optional linear projector ($\text{BRA}_{calib}$) trained offline via hard-negative mining to prevent data leakage; (2) **Bounded Spatio-Temporal Adaptive Top-$k$ Resonance**, isolating fine-grained $T \times H \times W$ features, mathematically managing the severe $O(N_v)$ VRAM *read* bandwidth bottleneck via fused Triton kernels and pre-fill contiguous caching; and (3) **Stateful Probabilistic Vocabulary-Anchored Semantic Masking (VASM)**, replacing brittle predictive entropy with an offline syntactic mask equipped with strict BPE continuation rules. We establish a rigorous 5-tier "Defense Line" experimental protocol—spanning absolute VRAM/latency profiling, dense OCR heatmaps, Temporal mIoU, and token-level log-probability tracking—designed to systematically validate the zero-pooling supremacy of BRA against both image and video-adapted baselines.

## 1. Introduction

A persistent challenge in Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs) is the competitive imbalance between language priors and visual evidence. During autoregressive decoding, when the statistical inertia of the language model overpowers the conditioned visual context, the model produces hallucinations—generating text that is linguistically fluent but physically or temporally absent from the multimedia input.

Current inference-time mitigation strategies generally bifurcate into two routes. The first relies on multi-pass contrastive decoding (e.g., VCD). While effective, these methods introduce severe latency overheads and VRAM bloat, rendering them computationally hostile to long-context generation. The second route employs hidden-space projection (e.g., DoLa). However, when applied to high-resolution images or long videos, these methods consistently encounter what we term the **Pooling Paradox**: to compute a unified visual direction for hidden-state subtraction, they forcefully pool (e.g., MeanPool, MaxPool, or PCA) thousands of visual tokens. This irreversible aggregation destroys the fine-grained spatial-temporal coordinates that the model relies on to ground microscopic dense text or fleeting temporal actions, leading to catastrophic failures in high-resolution visual question answering or specific video frame localization.

The fundamental premise of Bi-directional Resonance Anchoring (BRA) is to bypass the hidden space entirely and intervene at the terminal logits space. By computing a localized match between dynamic text candidate tokens and un-pooled visual tokens, BRA preserves exact spatial and temporal information. However, escaping the pooling paradox inherently introduces severe systemic trade-offs: comparing vast spatio-temporal volumes ($N_v \to 50,000$) against the vocabulary at every step not only explodes FLOPs but hits a massive memory bandwidth wall (reading $\approx 800$ MB/step from global VRAM). Furthermore, it exposes the sub-word fragmentation vulnerability where multi-token visual entities escape penalization.

This paper establishes the rigorous mathematical framework of BRA, bounded by strict memory bandwidth controls and a comprehensive, hypothesis-driven evaluation protocol. Our methodology features:
1. **Dual-Track Calibration & Representation Validation**: To ensure fair structural comparisons, we explicitly define $\text{BRA}_{zero}$ (pure zero-shot identity mapping, grounded by a mandatory $W_{vocab}$ vs. $h_L^{(v_j)}$ sanity check) alongside $\text{BRA}_{calib}$ (an offline-trained localized projector with strict data-leakage prevention). 
2. **Bandwidth-Aware Adaptive Resonance**: Transitioning from dangerous absolute max-pooling to a dynamic Top-$k$ formulation. Crucially, we justify using static visual features extracted during the pre-fill stage; while the visual cache remains frozen to prevent VRAM explosion, the step-by-step autoregressive dynamics are captured by the continuously evolving candidate set $\mathcal{C}_t$. Memory read bottlenecks are bypassed via custom fused Triton kernels.
3. **Stateful Probabilistic VASM**: Replacing flawed predictive entropy with an offline, polysemy-aware masking mechanism structurally upgraded with a BPE inheritance heuristic to protect multi-token entities across diverse tokenizers.
4. **The 5-Line Defense Protocol**: Expanding the experimental blueprint into a strict five-stage defense, explicitly tracking absolute VRAM consumption, False Suppression Rates, Temporal mIoU, and token-level log-probabilities to definitively prove the mathematical claims of the BRA framework.

## 2. Related Work

### 2.1 Inference-Time Intervention for MLLMs

针对 MLLM 幻觉的推理期方法大致可分为三类。第一类是对比解码，如 VCD 通过干净图像和噪声图像两路前向的 logits 差异进行校正。第二类是注意力级回溯方法，如 OPERA 通过监控注意力聚集模式，对过度信任的生成路径施加惩罚。第三类是层间对比方法，如 DoLa 通过成熟层与未成熟层的分布差异来进行对数空间干预。

这些方法的共同问题在于：它们往往以更高的推理代价换取更保守的输出。在高吞吐或实时系统中，这种代价很容易超过可部署边界。对于网页代理、GUI grounding 或长视频推理场景，几十毫秒到数秒级的额外延迟会直接破坏 OODA 闭环。

### 2.2 Representation Engineering and the Pooling Paradox

与解码搜索不同，隐藏状态干预强调从表征几何中找到“真实性方向”或“偏见方向”，并在推理期做最小幅度修正。这类方法在纯文本上富有启发性，但在高分辨率 MLLM 上遭遇新的结构性问题：视觉分支不再是一个紧凑的全局向量，而是保留大规模空间排列的 token 网格。如果为了做单向量干预而先进行均值池化或 token 压缩，那么最需要保留的局部位置与细粒度 OCR 线索就会在干预前被破坏。

我们将这种问题称为 pooling paradox：**为了修正 hallucination，先破坏 grounding。** BRA 的“zero-pooling logits-side intervention”正是对这一悖论的回应。

### 2.3 Temporal Hallucination in Video MLLMs

视频场景中的幻觉不只是“看见了不存在的物体”，还包括“看见了不存在的动作”。近年的工作已经开始强调动作-场景混淆、时间顺序错误以及长程事件因果失配。一个常见但危险的简化，是将时间维度扁平化为静态特征平均，或将相邻帧在绝对坐标上直接相减。这样的设计在存在 camera motion、zoom、pan 或主体位移时，很容易把背景变化误当成动作证据。

因此，视频版 BRA 的目标不应是简单叠加一条 temporal branch，而应是构造一个尽量保守、尽量抑制公共运动噪声的 temporal resonance。

## 3. Methodology

### 3.1 Bounded Candidate Filtering & Offline Modality Calibration ($\Phi$)

Comparing $N_v$ visual tokens against the entire vocabulary $|V|$ at every decoding step $t$ results in an intractable $O(|V| \times N_v \times d)$ complexity and catastrophic memory read overhead. To resolve this, we define a sharply bounded candidate set $\mathcal{C}_t = \text{Top-}M(L_{orig})$, where $M \ll |V|$ (e.g., $M=50$). We utilize the post-LLM contextualized hidden states corresponding to the visual token positions, denoted as $h_L^{(v_j)}$. 

While $h_L^{(v_j)}$ are extracted statically during the pre-fill phase to conserve compute, step-by-step autoregressive semantic shifts are dynamically captured by the evolving text candidate set $\mathcal{C}_t$. To compute resonance with the text unembedding matrix $W_{vocab}$, we formalize an alignment mapping $\Phi$:

1. **Zero-Shot BRA ($\text{BRA}_{zero}$)**: $\Phi$ is the identity function. This relies purely on the LLM's intrinsic representation alignment. To validate the precarious assumption that final-layer visual tokens align with $W_{vocab}$, we mandate a **Representation Sanity Check** (see Sec 4.1) prior to deployment. $\text{BRA}_{zero}$ serves as the primary fair baseline against methods like DoLa.
2. **Calibrated BRA ($\text{BRA}_{calib}$)**: A lightweight single-layer linear projector trained entirely offline to bridge disjointed visual and textual spaces. To prevent contextual bleed, we extract visual states ($h_{bbox}$) using strict RoIAlign cropping over ground-truth bounding boxes from the Visual Genome (VG) dataset. To prevent data leakage, all evaluation targets from POPE and CHAIR are strictly excluded from the VG training split. Let $c^+$ be the ground-truth text entity. Crucially, the hard negatives $\{c^-\}$ are not random vocabulary words; they are defined as *visually co-occurring but semantically distinct objects* within the same scene (e.g., sampling 'table' when the target is 'chair'):
$$
\mathcal{L}_{calib} = -\log \frac{\exp\left(\cos(W_{vocab}[c^+], \Phi_{calib}(h_{bbox})) / \tau\right)}{\sum_{c \in \{c^+\} \cup \{c^-\}} \exp\left(\cos(W_{vocab}[c], \Phi_{calib}(h_{bbox})) / \tau\right)}
$$
*Note on Temporal Generalization:* While $\Phi_{calib}$ is trained on 2D spatial data, it explicitly aligns *object semantics* rather than temporal dynamics. Therefore, it generalizes seamlessly to isolating objects within specific video frames ($T \times H \times W$ localization), though it inherently cannot reason about causal temporal motion.

### 3.2 Spatio-Temporal Adaptive Top-$k$ Resonance & Overcoming the Memory Bandwidth Wall

For continuous resolution models and videos, $N_v = T \times H \times W$ fluctuates drastically. Relying on global mean-pooling destroys spatial-temporal localization. We formulate **Spatio-Temporal Adaptive Top-$k$ Resonance**, defining an adaptive $k$ bounded by both a lower threshold (to avoid noise) and an upper threshold (to prevent re-introducing the pooling paradox in ultra-long videos):
$$
k = \min\Big(k_{max}, \max\big(k_{min}, \lceil \rho \cdot (T \times H \times W) \rceil\big)\Big)
$$
where $\rho$ is a small fractional threshold (e.g., $0.005$). The raw resonance score for a candidate $c \in \mathcal{C}_t$ is:
$$
S_{raw}(c) = \frac{1}{k} \sum_{j \in \text{Top-}k} \frac{\cos\left(W_{vocab}[c], \Phi(h_L^{(v_j)})\right)}{\tau_{sim}}
$$
**Overcoming the $O(N_v)$ Memory Read Bottleneck:** 
While the compute overhead is $\approx 10$ GFLOPs/step, the true hardware limit is the memory bandwidth wall: reading $N_v \approx 50,000$ tokens of dimension $d=4096$ at every autoregressive step requires streaming $\approx 800$ MB from global VRAM. 

BRA resolves this via **Pre-fill Contiguous Caching and Fused Kernels**. Inspired by PagedAttention, static $h_L^{(v_j)}$ are cloned into a strictly contiguous C++ tensor block during the pre-fill stage. During decoding, the Top-$k$ resonance is computed via a custom fused Triton kernel that performs batched GEMV and Top-$k$ reduction within SRAM, avoiding intermediate global memory roundtrips.

### 3.3 Relative Resonance Penalty 

We map raw resonance scores into stable logits modifications by computing the relative evidence position within the Top-$M$ candidates via a Softmax operation, bounding the score $\hat S(c) \in [0,1]$:
$$
\hat S(c) = \text{Softmax}\big(S_{raw}(c) \mid c \in \mathcal{C}_t\big)
$$
The final logits are reshaped via a unified **Relative Resonance Penalty**:
$$
L_{final}(c) = L_{orig}(c) - \alpha \cdot \mathbb{E}[\gamma(c_t)] \cdot (1 - \hat S(c))
$$

### 3.4 Escaping the Entropy Trap: Stateful Probabilistic VASM

Previous iterations used predictive entropy to scale penalties, creating an **"Entropy Trap"**: strong language priors inherently produce low-entropy predictions for hallucinated objects. We guarantee mathematical stability via **Stateful Probabilistic Vocabulary-Anchored Semantic Masking (VASM)**. 

We pre-compute a continuous syntactic weight based on POS probability distributions: $\mathbb{E}[\gamma(c)] = \sum P(pos \mid c) \cdot \gamma_{pos}$. To prevent a **Sub-word Fragmentation Vulnerability** (where fragmented visual entities like "re", "friger", "ator" default to $0.0$ and escape the penalty), VASM utilizes a **BPE Continuation Heuristic**:
If a token $c_t$ is morphologically identified as a sub-word continuation (e.g., lacking the `_` prefix in SentencePiece, or matching continuation byte-patterns in Tiktoken), it inherits the expected semantic weight of its root word:
$$
\mathbb{E}[\gamma(c_t)] = \begin{cases} 
\mathbb{E}[\gamma(c_{t-1})], & \text{if } c_t \text{ is a BPE continuation} \\ 
\sum P(pos \mid c_t) \cdot \gamma_{pos}, & \text{otherwise} 
\end{cases}
$$
This structurally protects functional syntax while rigorously penalizing unsupported multi-token visual entities.

## 4. Experimental Design and Evaluation Protocol

To definitively validate the structural advantages of BRA—and explicitly address the computational and fairness assumptions inherent in logits-space interventions—we define a rigorous, hypothesis-driven Evaluation Protocol. All planned experiments will be conducted across distinct model families (e.g., LLaVA-NeXT, Qwen-VL), isolating $\text{BRA}_{zero}$ vs. $\text{BRA}_{calib}$.

### 4.1 Preliminary Ablations & Representation Sanity Check
**Objective:** Prove the foundational assumption of $\text{BRA}_{zero}$ and validate systems-level memory/compute optimizations.
**Protocol:**
- **Representation Sanity Check:** Extract $h_L^{(v_j)}$ from the Base model. Compute and visualize a cosine similarity heatmap against $W_{vocab}$ for ground-truth objects. 
- **Absolute Latency & VRAM Profiling:** Report Time Per Output Token (ms/token) and explicitly track **Absolute VRAM Consumption (GB)** during the generation phase for $N_v \in \{1k, 10k, 50k\}$.
- **Hypothesis:** The sanity check will reveal a non-random, semantically clustered similarity distribution, mathematically authorizing $\text{BRA}_{zero}$. Systems profiling will demonstrate that BRA's Triton kernels and static pre-fill caching maintain a VRAM footprint identical to the Base model (unlike VCD's memory bloat), with a manageable TPOT ($<50$ ms/token at $N_v=50k$).

### 4.2 Defense Line 1: Core Hallucination Suppression & False Suppression Tracking
**Objective:** Prove $\text{BRA}_{zero}$ structurally mitigates object hallucinations without heavily sacrificing recall or relying on `<EOS>` hacking.
**Protocol:**
- **Benchmarks:** POPE, CHAIR (MSCOCO).
- **Baselines:** Base, VCD, OPERA, DoLa. MUST include both $\text{BRA}_{zero}$ and $\text{BRA}_{calib}$.
- **Key Metric Requirements:** F1 (POPE), CHAIR$_S$, **Average Generated Length (AGL)**, and **False Suppression Rate (FSR)** (tracking instances where a valid object was incorrectly penalized).
- **Hypothesis:** $\text{BRA}_{zero}$ will independently outperform DoLa/VCD while maintaining an AGL within 5% of Base. The FSR metric will prove that localized Top-$k$ resonance preserves true positives significantly better than mean-pooling strategies. 

### 4.3 Defense Line 2: General Intelligence & Language Prior Integrity
**Objective:** Ensure visual logit penalization does not lobotomize instructional compliance or cognitive reasoning.
**Protocol:**
- **Benchmarks:** MMBench V1.1, MME. Include VCD and DoLa explicitly.
- **Reporting:** Total score, isolating "Perception" vs. "Cognition/Reasoning".
- **Hypothesis:** Thanks to VASM shielding functional syntax ($\mathbb{E}[\gamma] \approx 0$), pure $\text{BRA}_{zero}$ will improve Perception. Crucially, explicitly comparing against VCD/DoLa will prove that while multi-pass/pooling methods severely degrade the "Cognition" subsets, BRA protects general intelligence.

### 4.4 Defense Line 3: "Zero-Pooling" Supremacy on Dense Spatial Tasks
**Objective:** Qualitatively and quantitatively expose the "Pooling Paradox" on high-resolution targets.
**Protocol:**
- **Benchmarks:** FREAK (OCR / Position subset) and DocVQA.
- **Spatial Heatmap Mandate:** Render a side-by-side visualization on a dense receipt: (1) Original image, (2) Standard LLM Cross-Attention / prompt attention baseline, (3) DoLa MeanPool activations, and (4) BRA Top-$k$ activations.
- **Hypothesis:** BRA's Adaptive Top-$k$ will dynamically produce sharp, isolated activation points precisely over the microscopic text patches being decoded, visually proving superiority over both mean-pooling (which blurs globally) and standard cross-attention (which often exhibits attention sink artifacts).

### 4.5 Defense Line 4: Spatio-Temporal Grounding in Video Narrative
**Objective:** Validate that Adaptive Top-$k$ scales across $T \times H \times W$ for exact frame localization.
**Protocol:**
- **Benchmarks:** VIDHALLUC and Video-MME (Localization subsets). Include Temporal-DoLa.
- **Metric Mandate:** Replace loose hit-rates with rigorous **Temporal mIoU** (mean Intersection over Union) between the Top-$k$ activation window and the ground-truth temporal window. Plot a Temporal Histogram overlaying BRA vs. Temporal-DoLa.
- **Hypothesis:** Temporal-DoLa will display a flat activation curve due to temporal averaging, resulting in low mIoU. BRA's tokens will autonomously spike into a sharp Gaussian peak strictly within the ground-truth window, yielding high Temporal mIoU. *(Note: Evaluates grounding, not causal sequential logic).*

### 4.6 Defense Line 5: VASM Ablation & The Entropy Trap
**Objective:** Mathematically expose the "Entropy Trap" and analyze algorithmic edge-case failures.
**Protocol:**
- **Benchmarks:** MMMU (Hard Subset) for VASM ablation.
- **Metric Mandate:** Extract specific **token-level log-probabilities** of hallucinated entities vs. syntactic tokens with and without VASM.
- **Hypothesis:** The token-level log-prob extraction will definitively show that without VASM ($\mathbb{E}[\gamma] = 1.0$), the penalty disproportionately destroys low-entropy structural syntax (crashing MMMU scores). VASM will successfully redirect the penalty exclusively to high-entropy semantic nouns.

## 5. Discussion & Limitations

The architectural shift from hidden-state subtraction to localized logits-space resonance anchoring resolves a critical structural bottleneck in high-resolution MLLMs. By explicitly addressing the memory bandwidth wall via fused Triton kernels, strictly defining negative sampling to avoid data leakage, and systematically validating representation assumptions, BRA provides a mathematically robust pathway to intervene safely without destroying fragile 2D/3D encodings. 

**Limitations & Future Work:**
1. **Static Pre-fill Visual Caching:** While extracting static visual features ($h_L^{(v_j)}$) during the pre-fill stage successfully prevents VRAM explosion and memory read bottlenecks, it inherently freezes the visual representation. Although the autoregressive dynamics are captured by the evolving text candidate set $\mathcal{C}_t$, future work will explore lightweight, dynamically updating visual caches to better handle heavily compositional generation.
2. **Temporal Naivety (Bag-of-Frames):** Treating $T \times H \times W$ as a unified volume is highly effective for spatio-temporal *grounding* (Temporal mIoU), but it ignores causal temporal order, limiting efficacy on causal video reasoning tasks.

## 6. Revised Claims (Pending Empirical Execution)
Until the 5-Tier Defense Protocol is fully executed, we limit our claims to the theoretical and algorithmic soundness of the framework. We do not claim arbitrary SOTA performance. Our contribution stands as a rigorously formulated protocol designed to expose the specific spatial-temporal bottlenecks of MLLM interventions, offering a mathematically bounded, memory-aware, and BPE-safe blueprint to validate zero-pooling decoding across static and dynamic multimedia architectures.
