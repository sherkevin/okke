# Bi-directional Resonance Anchoring: Escaping the Pooling Paradox in High-Resolution Image and Video MLLMs

## Abstract
Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs) frequently suffer from hallucinations, generating text disconnected from visual or temporal evidence due to overwhelming language priors. Existing hidden-state interventions often fall into the "Pooling Paradox": to apply unified directional corrections, they aggregate thousands of high-resolution spatial or temporal tokens, inadvertently destroying the fine-grained 2D/3D positional coordinates necessary for dense grounding (e.g., Document VQA) and specific frame localization (e.g., Video Action). In this paper, we propose the mathematical framework and evaluation blueprint for **Bi-directional Resonance Anchoring (BRA)**, a decode-time intervention operating directly in the terminal logits space via localized visual matching. 

To resolve the compute and memory-bandwidth bottlenecks inherent in zero-pooling logits interventions, BRA introduces three systemic upgrades: (1) **Dual-Track Modality Calibration ($\Phi$)**, establishing a zero-shot identity mapping ($\text{BRA}_{zero}$) validated via representation sanity checks, alongside an optional, structurally trivial single-layer projector ($\text{BRA}_{calib}$) trained offline via hard-negative mining; (2) **Dynamic Top-$p$ Candidate Filtering & Confidence-Gated Adaptive Top-$k$ Resonance**, which mathematically isolates fine-grained $T \times H \times W$ features, bypasses the Softmax noise-amplification trap, and manages the severe $O(N_v)$ VRAM *read* bandwidth bottleneck via fused Triton kernels and pre-fill contiguous caching; and (3) **Stateful Probabilistic Vocabulary-Anchored Semantic Masking (VASM)**, replacing brittle predictive entropy with an offline syntactic mask equipped with strict BPE continuation rules. We establish a rigorous 5-tier Defense Line protocol—spanning absolute VRAM scaling charts, False Suppression Rates (FSR), dense OCR heatmaps, Temporal mIoU, and token-level log-probability tracking—designed to systematically validate the zero-pooling supremacy of BRA across both image and video modalities.

## 1. Introduction

A persistent challenge in Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs) is the competitive imbalance between language priors and visual evidence. During autoregressive decoding, when the statistical inertia of the language model overpowers the conditioned visual context, the model produces hallucinations—generating text that is linguistically fluent but physically or temporally absent from the multimedia input.

Current inference-time mitigation strategies generally bifurcate into two routes. The first relies on multi-pass contrastive decoding (e.g., VCD). While effective, these methods introduce severe latency overheads and VRAM bloat, rendering them computationally hostile to long-context generation. The second route employs hidden-space projection (e.g., DoLa). However, when applied to high-resolution images or long videos, these methods consistently encounter what we term the **Pooling Paradox**: to compute a unified visual direction for hidden-state subtraction, they forcefully pool (e.g., MeanPool, MaxPool, or PCA) thousands of visual tokens. This irreversible aggregation destroys the fine-grained spatial-temporal coordinates that the model relies on to ground microscopic dense text or fleeting temporal actions, leading to catastrophic failures in high-resolution visual question answering or specific video frame localization.

The fundamental premise of Bi-directional Resonance Anchoring (BRA) is to bypass the hidden space entirely and intervene at the terminal logits space. By computing a localized match between dynamic text candidate tokens and un-pooled visual tokens, BRA preserves exact spatial and temporal information. However, escaping the pooling paradox inherently introduces severe systemic trade-offs: comparing vast spatio-temporal volumes ($N_v \to 50,000$) against the vocabulary at every step not only explodes FLOPs but hits a massive memory bandwidth wall (reading $\approx 800$ MB/step from global VRAM). Furthermore, intervening in logits exposes the intervention to sub-word fragmentation vulnerabilities and noise-amplification loops.

This paper establishes the rigorous mathematical framework of BRA, bounded by strict memory bandwidth controls and a comprehensive, hypothesis-driven evaluation protocol. Our methodology features:
1. **Dynamic Nucleus Filtering & Dual-Track Calibration**: To prevent target clipping, we dynamically bound candidates using Top-$p$ nucleus filtering. We define $\text{BRA}_{zero}$ (pure zero-shot identity mapping) alongside $\text{BRA}_{calib}$ (an ultra-lightweight offline projector with strict data-leakage prevention).
2. **Confidence-Gated Spatio-Temporal Resonance**: Transitioning from dangerous absolute max-pooling to a dynamic Top-$k$ formulation with a hard noise-gating threshold to prevent hallucinated words from being artificially boosted. Memory read bottlenecks are bypassed via custom fused Triton kernels operating on static visual pre-fill caches.
3. **Stateful Probabilistic VASM**: Replacing flawed predictive entropy with an offline, polysemy-aware masking mechanism structurally upgraded with a BPE inheritance heuristic to protect multi-token entities across diverse tokenizers.
4. **The 5-Line Defense Protocol**: Expanding the experimental blueprint into a strict five-stage defense, explicitly tracking absolute VRAM consumption, False Suppression Rates (FSR), Dense OCR spatial heatmaps, Temporal mIoU, and token-level log-probabilities to definitively prove the zero-pooling claims.

## 2. Related Work

### 2.1 Inference-Time Intervention for MLLMs

针对 MLLM 幻觉的推理期方法大致可分为三类。第一类是对比解码，如 VCD 通过干净图像和噪声图像两路前向的 logits 差异进行校正。第二类是注意力级回溯方法，如 OPERA 通过监控注意力聚集模式，对过度信任的生成路径施加惩罚。第三类是层间对比方法，如 DoLa 通过成熟层与未成熟层的分布差异来进行对数空间干预。

这些方法的共同问题在于：它们往往以更高的推理代价换取更保守的输出。在高吞吐或实时系统中，这种代价很容易超过可部署边界。对于网页代理、GUI grounding 或长视频推理场景，几十毫秒到数秒级的额外延迟会直接破坏 OODA 闭环。

### 2.2 Representation Engineering and the Pooling Paradox

与解码搜索不同，隐藏状态干预强调从表征几何中找到“真实性方向”或“偏见方向”，并在推理期做最小幅度修正。这类方法在纯文本上富有启发性，但在高分辨率 MLLM 上遭遇新的结构性问题：视觉分支不再是一个紧凑的全局向量，而是保留大规模空间排列的 token 网格。如果为了做单向量干预而先进行均值池化或 token 压缩，那么最需要保留的局部位置与细粒度 OCR 线索就会在干预前被破坏。

我们将这种问题称为 pooling paradox：**为了修正 hallucination，先破坏 grounding。** BRA 的“zero-pooling logits-side intervention”正是对这一悖论的回应。

### 2.3 Temporal Hallucination in Video MLLMs

视频场景中的幻觉不只是“看见了不存在的物体”，还包括“看见了不存在的动作”。近年的工作已经开始强调动作-场景混淆、时间顺序错误以及长程事件因果失配。一个常见但危险的简化，是将时间维度扁平化为静态特征平均，或将相邻帧在绝对坐标上直接相减。这样的设计在存在 camera motion、zoom、pan 或主体位移时，很容易把背景变化误当成动作证据。

因此，视频版 BRA 的目标不应是简单叠加一条 temporal branch，而应是构造一个尽量保守、尽量抑制公共运动噪声的 temporal resonance。

## 3. Methodology

### 3.1 Dynamic Top-$p$ Candidate Filtering & Modality Calibration ($\Phi$)

Comparing $N_v$ visual tokens against the entire vocabulary $|V|$ at every decoding step $t$ results in an intractable $O(|V| \times N_v \times d)$ complexity and catastrophic memory read overhead. To resolve this, we dynamically bound the candidate set $\mathcal{C}_t$. Instead of a static Top-$M$ cut-off (which risks clipping the true visual entity if the model is uncertain), we employ **Dynamic Nucleus Filtering (Top-$p$)**: $\mathcal{C}_t = \{c \in V \mid \sum P(c) \le p\}$, capped by a generous upper bound $M_{max}$ to ensure latency stability. We utilize the post-LLM contextualized hidden states corresponding to the visual token positions, denoted as $h_L^{(v_j)}$. 

To compute resonance with the text unembedding matrix $W_{vocab}$, we formalize an alignment mapping $\Phi$:

1. **Zero-Shot BRA ($\text{BRA}_{zero}$)**: $\Phi$ is the identity function, relying purely on the LLM's intrinsic representation alignment. To validate the assumption that final-layer visual tokens loosely align with $W_{vocab}$, we mandate a **Representation Sanity Check** prior to deployment.
2. **Calibrated BRA ($\text{BRA}_{calib}$)**: A structurally trivial single-layer linear projector ($\sim 16$M parameters, negligible FLOPs overhead) trained entirely offline to bridge disjointed spaces. To prevent contextual bleed, we extract visual states via a strict $7 \times 7$ RoIAlign crop over ground-truth bounding boxes from the Visual Genome (VG) dataset. To prevent object co-occurrence data leakage into downstream benchmarks (e.g., MMBench), all evaluation targets from POPE and CHAIR are excluded. Crucially, the hard negatives $\{c^-\}$ are not random words; they are visually co-occurring but semantically distinct objects:
$$
\mathcal{L}_{calib} = -\log \frac{\exp\left(\cos(W_{vocab}[c^+], \Phi_{calib}(h_{bbox})) / \tau\right)}{\sum_{c \in \{c^+\} \cup \{c^-\}} \exp\left(\cos(W_{vocab}[c], \Phi_{calib}(h_{bbox})) / \tau\right)}
$$
*Note on Temporal Generalization:* While $\Phi_{calib}$ is trained on 2D spatial data, it aligns *object semantics* rather than temporal dynamics. Therefore, it mathematically generalizes to isolating objects within specific video frames ($T \times H \times W$ localization as a unified volume), fully justifying our dual-modality (Image+Video) deployment.

### 3.2 Confidence-Gated Spatio-Temporal Adaptive Top-$k$ Resonance

For continuous resolution models and videos, $N_v = T \times H \times W$ fluctuates drastically. Relying on global mean-pooling destroys spatial-temporal localization. We formulate **Spatio-Temporal Adaptive Top-$k$ Resonance**, defining an adaptive $k$ bounded by lower/upper thresholds to prevent noise amplification and re-introducing the pooling paradox:
$$
k = \min\Big(k_{max}, \max\big(k_{min}, \lceil \rho \cdot (T \times H \times W) \rceil\big)\Big)
$$
where $\rho$ is a density scalar (e.g., $0.005$). The raw resonance score for a candidate $c \in \mathcal{C}_t$ is:
$$
S_{raw}(c) = \frac{1}{k} \sum_{j \in \text{Top-}k} \frac{\cos\left(W_{vocab}[c], \Phi(h_L^{(v_j)})\right)}{\tau_{sim}}
$$
**Overcoming the $O(N_v)$ Memory Read Bottleneck:** 
Reading $N_v \approx 50,000$ tokens of dimension $d=4096$ at every step requires streaming $\approx 800$ MB from global VRAM. BRA resolves this via **Pre-fill Contiguous Caching and Fused Kernels**. Static $h_L^{(v_j)}$ are cloned into a strictly contiguous C++ tensor block during the pre-fill stage. During decoding, Top-$k$ resonance is computed via a custom fused Triton kernel performing batched GEMV and Top-$k$ reduction purely within SRAM.

### 3.3 Confidence-Gated Relative Penalty 

Mapping raw resonance into stable logits modifications requires care. If a hallucinated word (e.g., "dinosaur") yields purely noise-level similarities across all visual patches, applying a standard Softmax across $\mathcal{C}_t$ can inadvertently boost a random patch to $\hat S \approx 1.0$. We introduce a **Minimum Confidence Gate ($\tau_{noise}$)**:
$$
\hat S(c) = \begin{cases} 
0, & \text{if } \max_{j}(S_{raw}(c)) < \tau_{noise} \\
\text{Softmax}\big(S_{raw}(c) \mid c \in \mathcal{C}_t\big), & \text{otherwise}
\end{cases}
$$
The final logits are reshaped via a dynamically scaled penalty based on the maximum confidence within the candidate set ($S_{max} = \max_{c} \hat{S}(c)$):
$$
L_{final}(c) = L_{orig}(c) - \alpha \cdot (1 - S_{max}) \cdot \mathbb{E}[\gamma(c_t)] \cdot (1 - \hat S(c))
$$
This ensures that if no candidate matches the visual evidence strongly, the intervention scales down gracefully rather than scrambling the distribution.

### 3.4 Escaping the Entropy Trap: Stateful Probabilistic VASM

Previous logits interventions heavily relied on predictive entropy to scale penalties, creating an **"Entropy Trap"**: strong language priors inherently produce low-entropy predictions for hallucinated objects, nullifying the penalty exactly when it is needed most. 

We guarantee mathematical stability via **Stateful Probabilistic Vocabulary-Anchored Semantic Masking (VASM)**. We pre-compute a continuous syntactic weight based on POS probability distributions: $\mathbb{E}[\gamma(c)] = \sum P(pos \mid c) \cdot \gamma_{pos}$. To prevent the **Sub-word Fragmentation Vulnerability** (where fragmented visual entities like "re", "friger", "ator" default to $0.0$ and escape the penalty), VASM utilizes a **BPE Continuation Heuristic**:
$$
\mathbb{E}[\gamma(c_t)] = \begin{cases} 
\mathbb{E}[\gamma(c_{t-1})], & \text{if } c_t \text{ is a BPE continuation (e.g., lacking `_` or matching Tiktoken byte-patterns)} \\ 
\sum P(pos \mid c_t) \cdot \gamma_{pos}, & \text{otherwise} 
\end{cases}
$$
This structurally protects functional syntax while rigorously penalizing unsupported multi-token visual entities.

## 4. Evaluation Blueprint & The 5-Line Defense Protocol

To definitively validate the structural advantages of BRA—and explicitly address the computational and fairness assumptions inherent in logits-space interventions—we define a rigorous, hypothesis-driven Evaluation Protocol. All planned experiments will be conducted across distinct model families (e.g., LLaVA-NeXT, Qwen-VL). 

### 4.1 Defense Line 1: Sanity Checks & Core Hallucination Suppression
**Objective:** Prove system-level viability and that BRA suppresses hallucinations without indiscriminately penalizing true objects.
**Protocol:**
- **Systems Profiling (VRAM vs Context Length):** Produce a line chart tracking absolute generation-phase VRAM (GB) across $N_v \in \{1k, 10k, 50k\}$. **Hypothesis:** VCD will exponentially diverge (memory bloat), while BRA's Triton kernels will maintain a flat line perfectly overlapping the Base model.
- **Core Suppression:** Evaluate POPE and CHAIR. Must explicitly report the **False Suppression Rate (FSR)**—tracking instances where a valid object was incorrectly penalized.
- **Hypothesis:** $\text{BRA}_{zero}$ will suppress hallucinations while maintaining a minimal FSR, proving that localized Top-$k$ resonance preserves true positives significantly better than mean-pooling strategies.

### 4.2 Defense Line 2: General Intelligence & Language Prior Integrity
**Objective:** Ensure visual logit penalization does not lobotomize instructional compliance or cognitive reasoning.
**Protocol:**
- **Benchmarks:** MMBench V1.1 and MME. Tables must explicitly isolate $\text{BRA}_{zero}$ vs $\text{BRA}_{calib}$ to isolate the impact of offline training.
- **Hypothesis:** Explicitly comparing against VCD/DoLa will prove that while multi-pass/pooling methods severely degrade "Cognition" subsets, BRA protects general intelligence thanks to VASM shielding functional syntax ($\mathbb{E}[\gamma] \approx 0$).

### 4.3 Defense Line 3: "Zero-Pooling" Supremacy on Dense Spatial Tasks
**Objective:** Qualitatively and quantitatively expose the "Pooling Paradox" on high-resolution targets.
**Protocol:**
- **Benchmarks:** FREAK (OCR / Position subset) and DocVQA.
- **Spatial Heatmap Mandate:** Render a side-by-side visualization on a high-res receipt. Draw a red circle around a tiny $10 \times 10$ pixel text patch being decoded.
- **Hypothesis:** DoLa's mean-pooling activations will globally smear across the document. BRA's Adaptive Top-$k$ will dynamically produce sharp, isolated activation points precisely inside the red circle, visually proving the "Pooling Paradox" claim.

### 4.4 Defense Line 4: Spatio-Temporal Grounding in Video Narrative
**Objective:** Validate that Adaptive Top-$k$ mathematically scales across $T \times H \times W$ for exact frame localization, justifying the Image+Video dual-track narrative.
**Protocol:**
- **Benchmarks:** VIDHALLUC and Video-MME (Localization subsets). Include Temporal-DoLa.
- **Metric Mandate:** Report rigorous **Temporal mIoU** between the Top-$k$ activation window and ground-truth frames. Plot a **Temporal Histogram** (Activation strength on Y-axis vs. Frame index on X-axis).
- **Hypothesis:** Temporal-DoLa will display a flat/wavy activation curve due to temporal averaging (low mIoU). BRA will autonomously spike into a sharp Gaussian peak strictly within the ground-truth window, proving superior spatio-temporal grounding.

### 4.5 Defense Line 5: VASM Ablation & The Entropy Trap
**Objective:** Mathematically expose the "Entropy Trap" and analyze algorithmic edge-case failures.
**Protocol:**
- **Benchmarks:** MMMU (Hard Subset) for VASM ablation.
- **Metric Mandate:** Extract specific **token-level log-probabilities** of complex logical prompts (e.g., containing words like "therefore", "integrate", "equals").
- **Hypothesis:** Pure predictive entropy penalizes syntactic connectors arbitrarily, crashing MMMU scores. Token-level logs will prove VASM successfully forces the penalty to zero for these structural tokens, redirecting the penalty exclusively to high-entropy semantic nouns.

## 5. Discussion & Limitations

The architectural shift from hidden-state subtraction to localized logits-space resonance anchoring resolves a critical structural bottleneck in high-resolution MLLMs. By explicitly addressing the memory bandwidth wall via fused Triton kernels, dynamically bounding candidates via Nucleus Filtering to prevent target clipping, and implementing confidence-gating to suppress noise amplification, BRA provides a mathematically robust pathway to intervene safely without destroying fragile 2D/3D encodings. 

**Limitations & Future Work:**
1. **Static Pre-fill Visual Caching:** Extracting static visual features ($h_L^{(v_j)}$) during the pre-fill stage successfully prevents VRAM explosion and memory read bottlenecks, but inherently freezes the visual representation. Future work will explore lightweight, dynamically updating visual caches to better handle heavily compositional generation.
2. **Temporal Naivety (Bag-of-Frames):** Treating $T \times H \times W$ as a unified volume is highly effective for spatio-temporal *grounding* (evidenced by Temporal mIoU hypotheses), but it mathematically ignores causal temporal order, limiting its efficacy on complex sequential video reasoning tasks.

## 6. Revised Claims (Pending Empirical Execution)
Until the 5-Tier Defense Protocol is fully executed, we limit our claims to the theoretical and algorithmic soundness of the framework. We do not claim arbitrary SOTA performance. Our contribution stands as a rigorously formulated protocol designed to expose the specific spatial-temporal bottlenecks of MLLM interventions, offering a mathematically bounded, memory-aware, and BPE-safe blueprint to validate zero-pooling decoding across static and dynamic multimedia architectures.
